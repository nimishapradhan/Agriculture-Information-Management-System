Agriculture Information Management System(AIMS) is developed by a group of students of Herald College Kathmandu. AIMS is a pioneering step toward modernizing the agriculture sector in Nepal.

AIMS is a virtual platform for all the farmers to update details of the crops they are growing for commercial purposes. Farmers can provide details of crops that include crop name, production, growth, fertilizers, reproduction, crop cycle, seasons, farmer rate, and province. The data provided by the farmers will be used for educational or research purposes.
